ROTATOR

An efficient rota organizer for groups: Get more points to have fewer responsibilities!

Have you ever complained that you do too much work for it or that your mates are always messy and careless? Surely you did, so this game-based application is the best opportunity for you to solve the issue! Tasks are assigned to each of the members in a group and they need to be solved by a given deadline. The more efficient you are, the more your score increases!

Ask yourself: “Who does the work in my flat?” and join ROTATOR now!
